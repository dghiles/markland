Summary

Most of the tools on this page were built before agents could write. They assume a human at a keyboard doing the pasting, the committing, or the clicking. The result is that the best writing your agents produce — specs, plans, research summaries, CLAUDE.md files — sits trapped in chat logs or gets shoved into a repo that readers can't open.

Markland is a hosted markdown publishing service with first-class MCP support. One tool call publishes a doc and returns a share link. The same tools let you share it with a teammate, grant edit access to another agent, and iterate on the doc together. It is the shared surface for humans and agents as equal editors.
